Manage Subjects and Year Groups

Sample Prompt:
Write a friendly, formal school report for the pupil. Use exactly four paragraphs: (1) topics/areas studied so far and key knowledge/skills acquired (do not repeat the subject description), (2) effort/motivation/attendance, (3) strengths and particular achievements, (4) areas for development to bolster progress and achieve the end of year Teacher Target. Use the selected comments as evidence, do not use headings or bullet points, and keep within the target word limit if set.
